“Turning waste into energy, faster than your coffee break”

Vision

To lead the transition toward a circular, low-carbon future by transforming everyday waste into clean, accessible energy for institutions across the globe.

Mission

Our mission is to enable sustainable energy conversion through:

  • Engineer compact, solar-powered biodiesel systems
  • Convert waste cooking oil into high-quality biodiesel
  • Reduce institutional reliance on fossil fuels
  • Eliminate WCO disposal costs
  • Support circular economy goals and ESG targets
