Our Milestone

Our journey began when we won the Renewable Energy Technical Incubator (RETI) competition, securing more than 50,000 SAR in funding. We dedicated these funds to advancing our R&D, building a functional prototype using a small-scale machinery unit, and validating our technology. Our unique selling point, which transforms waste into value through a cost-effective and eco-friendly production process, played a key role in helping us win the competition and secure the prize.

At the DeepTech Venture Builder Bootcamp organized by Dhahran Techno Valley (DTV), our team was selected as one of the top 10 and secured 200,000 SAR in funding. This support enables us to participate in the start-up incubator program, learn about real market supply-and-demand principles, study intellectual property, automate the machinery unit we have already developed, and conduct pilot testing for our product. We also expanded our network significantly, building strong relationships with suppliers, buyers, and investors.

Our Initiatives

At the BIBAN entrepreneurship exhibition, our team proudly showcased our innovative waste-to-energy solutions to a diverse audience of entrepreneurs, industry experts, and potential partners. Through this event, we gained valuable visibility and practical insights into the ecosystem, expanded our professional network across the region, and connected with key stakeholders in sustainability and technology. Our participation at BIBAN helped drive interest in our start-up and further strengthened our mission to scale impactful, circular bioenergy solutions.

We had the privilege to host the Ministry of Energy at our Dhahran Techno Valley (DTV) office, where we presented our innovative waste-to-biodiesel solution and demonstrated its potential for advancing the Kingdom's energy and sustainability goals. The Ministry expressed strong interest and full support for what we do, recognizing its role in promoting local innovation, managing waste, and enhancing a circular economy in Saudi Arabia. This engagement marks a significant milestone in our mission to develop impactful, green solutions through collaborative partnerships.
